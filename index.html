Karan Malhotra

Seasoned Project Manager and Quality Professional

Karan Malhotra is a seasoned project manager and quality professional with over 15 years of experience in quality assurance, project management, compliance audits, and lean methodologies. He is certified by the Project Management Institute and GBMP, and is a member of the American Society of Quality. Karan has implemented strategic projects, Quality Management Systems (ISO 9001, IATF 16949), and Lean systems globally, resulting in measurable advancements in customer satisfaction, product quality, and cost savings.

Karan holds a degree in Mechanical Engineering from PTU (India), a post-grad in Project Management from Humber (Canada), a Certification in Organizational Leadership from Harvard Business School (US), and a Certification in Global Supply Chain Management from Wharton School of Business (US).
